Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mentivo collects, uses, and protects information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and related services.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app and features you use, we may process app usage data, purchase status, subscription status, device language, app settings, crash diagnostics, advertising identifiers where permitted, and support messages you send to us.

Oracle Ball AI may process the question text you submit so the backend can generate an answer. We do not store OpenAI API keys inside the mobile app.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate app features, provide customer support, process purchases, prevent abuse, improve app quality, show ads to free users where enabled, and comply with legal obligations.

Analytics and Advertising

Our apps may use analytics and advertising SDKs such as Firebase, PostHog, Google AdMob, or Apple StoreKit. Analytics events should not include full question text. Advertising partners may process device and ad interaction data according to their own policies.

AI Processing

For AI-powered features, submitted text may be sent from the app to our backend and then to an AI provider for response generation. We avoid sending unnecessary personal information and use the data only to provide the requested feature and maintain service safety.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. We may receive transaction status and product identifiers needed to unlock paid features.

Data Retention

Local app history may remain on your device until you delete it. Backend records, if enabled for an app, are retained only as needed to operate the service, prevent abuse, handle support requests, and meet legal requirements.

Your Choices

You can delete local history in app settings, request account or data deletion, manage subscriptions through Apple, disable notifications, and control tracking permissions through iOS settings.

Children

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can review and remove it where required.

International Use

Our services may be used globally. Data may be processed in countries different from your country of residence, subject to applicable law and provider safeguards.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@mentivo.net.
