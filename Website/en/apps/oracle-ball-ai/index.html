FAQ

Oracle Ball AI questions

What is Oracle Ball AI?

Oracle Ball AI is a personal AI oracle for questions, daily horoscope-inspired guidance, self-reflection, and decision clarity.

Does Oracle Ball AI predict the future?

No. It offers guidance for reflection and personal orientation, not guaranteed predictions.

Can I ask by voice?

Yes. Voice input turns speech into text before the answer is generated.

What is Daily Oracle?

Daily Oracle is a once-per-day reading with horoscope, mood, energy, focus, advice, small action, question of the day, and lunar context where available.

Is the widget useful without Premium?

Yes. Free Lite keeps useful daily guidance. Premium keeps the richer full widget every day.

Can I buy Premium from the widget?

No. Purchases happen only inside the app through Apple purchase flows.
