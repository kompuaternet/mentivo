Delete Data

Use this page to understand how to remove data from Mentivo apps.

Delete Data Inside the App

Open the app, go to Settings, and use Delete question history or Delete account / data. This removes local app data from your device where the feature is available.

Request Backend Data Deletion

If an app account or backend storage is enabled, email mentivonet@gmail.com with the app name and the Apple account email or user identifier shown in the app settings, if available.

What We May Keep

We may keep records required for legal, tax, purchase verification, fraud prevention, security, or dispute resolution purposes where permitted by law.

Response Time

We aim to respond to deletion requests as soon as possible and within the time required by applicable law.
